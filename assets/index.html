Zinger Burger

Ingredients

2 chicken fillets
1 cup flour
1 tsp red chili powder
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 egg
1/2 cup milk
Burger buns
Mayo, lettuce, ketchup

Steps

 Mix flour + spices.
Dip chicken in egg & milk mixture, then coat with flour.
Deep fry until golden and crispy.
Toast buns lightly.
Add mayo, lettuce, crispy chicken, and ketchup.
Serve hot with fries 🍟
