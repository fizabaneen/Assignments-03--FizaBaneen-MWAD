Crispy Zinger Burger

Ingredients
    2 chicken fillets 1 cup flour 1 tsp red chili powder 1 tsp salt 1 tsp black pepper 1 egg 1/2 cup milk Burger buns Mayo, lettuce, ketchup

Steps
    Mix flour + spices
    Dip chicken in egg & milk mixture, then coat with flour
    Deep fry until golden and crispy
    Toast buns lightly
    Add mayo, lettuce, crispy chicken, and ketchup

    Serve hot with fries 🍟


Pizza

Ingredients
    2 cups flour 1 tsp yeast 1 tsp sugar 1/2 tsp salt Warm water Pizza sauce Cheese Chiken/veggies toppings

Method
    Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water
    Rest 5 mins
    Add flour + salt and make soft dough
    Rest 1 hour
    Roll dough into pizza shape Spread pizza sauce
    Add cheese and toppings
    Add cheese and toppings
    Bake for 15–20 mins until cheese melts and crust turns golden

    Enjoy hot cheesy pizza 🍕✨




Crispy French Fries

Ingredients
    3 potatoes Salt Oil for frying 1/2 tsp salt

Method
    Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips
    Soak in cold water for 20 mins
    Roll dough into pizza shape
    Fry on medium heat until light golden
    Fry again on high heat for extra crispiness
    Sprinkle salt and seasoning

    Serve hot with ketchup 🍅🍟✨




Chicken Spaghetti

Ingredients
    1 packet spaghetti 1 cup chicken (boneless) 2 tomatoes 2 tbsp ketchup 1 tsp garlic Salt & black pepper Chili flakes Oil

Method
    Boil spaghetti until soft
    Cook chicken with garlic and oil
    Add tomatoes, ketchup, salt, pepper, and chili flakes
    Cook sauce for 5–7 mins
    Add boiled spaghetti and mix well

    Serve hot with cheese if you like 🧀🍝




Crispy Samosa

Ingredients
    Samosa wrappers 2 potatoes (boiled) 1 green chili Salt Red chili powder Coriander Oil for frying

Method
    Mash potatoes and mix with spices & coriander
    Fill wrappers with potato mixture
    Fold into triangle shape and seal edges
    Deep fry until golden brown

    Serve hot with chutney or ketchup 🌶️🥟




Crispy Chicken Broast

Ingredients
    4 chicken pieces 1 cup flour 1 tsp salt 1 tsp red chili powder 1 tsp black pepper 1 tsp garlic powder 1 egg 1/2 cup milk Oil for frying

Method
    Mix flour and all spices
    Beat egg with milk in another bowl
    Dip chicken in egg mixture, then coat with flour
    Fry on medium heat until crispy and fully cooked

    Serve hot with fries and garlic sauce 🍟🍗




Beef Nihari

Ingredients
    1/2 kg beef 1 onion sliced 2 tbsp nihari masala 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste 4 cups water 2 tbsp flour Oil or ghee Salt to taste

Method
    Fry onion in oil until golden
    Add ginger garlic paste and beef
    Cook for 5 mins
    Add nihari masala, salt, and water
    Cook on low heat until meat becomes tender
    Mix flour with water and add for thick gravy
    Cook 10 more mins

    Garnish with ginger, coriander, green chili, and lemon 🍋🍲




Chicken Biryani

Ingredients
    1/2 kg chicken 2 cups rice 2 onions sliced 2 tomatoes 1 cup yogurt 2 tbsp biryani masala 2 potatoes (optional) Oil Green chilies & coriander

Method
    Fry onions until golden
    Add chicken and cook for 5 mins
    Add tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala
    Cook well
    Boil rice until 70% cooked
    Layer rice over chicken masala
    Add fried onions, coriander, and green chilies
    Cover and cook on low heat for 15–20 mins

    Serve hot with raita and salad 😋🍛
