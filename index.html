Crispy Zinger Burger

Ingredients
  • 2 chicken fillets
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Burger buns
  • Mayo, lettuce, ketchup

Steps
  1. Mix flour + spices.
  2. Dip chicken in egg & milk mixture, then coat with flour.
  3. Deep fry until golden and crispy.
  4. Toast buns lightly.
  5. Add mayo, lettuce, crispy chicken, and ketchup.

    6. Serve hot with fries 🍟


Pizza

Ingredients
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Warm water
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Chiken/veggies toppings

Method
  1. Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Rest 5 mins.
  2. Add flour + salt and make soft dough. Rest 1 hour.
  3. Roll dough into pizza shape
  4. Spread pizza sauce.
  5. Add cheese and toppings.Add cheese and toppings.
  6. Bake for 15–20 mins until cheese melts and crust turns golden.

    7. Enjoy hot cheesy pizza 🍕✨




Crispy French Fries

Ingredients
  • 3 potatoes
  • Salt
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Method
  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Soak in cold water for 20 mins.
  3. Roll dough into pizza shape.
  4. Fry on medium heat until light golden.
  5. Fry again on high heat for extra crispiness.
  6. Sprinkle salt and seasoning.

    7. Serve hot with ketchup 🍅🍟✨




Chicken Spaghetti

Ingredients
  • 1 packet spaghetti
  • 1 cup chicken (boneless)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Chili flakes
  • Oil

Method
  1. Boil spaghetti until soft.
  2. Cook chicken with garlic and oil.
  3. Add tomatoes, ketchup, salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  4. Cook sauce for 5–7 mins.
  5. Add boiled spaghetti and mix well.

    6. Serve hot with cheese if you like 🧀🍝




Crispy Samosa

Ingredients
  • Samosa wrappers
  • 2 potatoes (boiled)
  • 1 green chili
  • Salt
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander
  • Oil for frying

Method
  1. Mash potatoes and mix with spices & coriander.
  2. Fill wrappers with potato mixture.
  3. Fold into triangle shape and seal edges.
  4. Deep fry until golden brown.

    5. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup 🌶️🥟




Crispy Chicken Broast

Ingredients
  • 4 chicken pieces
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Oil for frying

Method
  1. Mix flour and all spices.
  2. Beat egg with milk in another bowl.
  3. Dip chicken in egg mixture, then coat with flour.
  4. Fry on medium heat until crispy and fully cooked.

    5. Serve hot with fries and garlic sauce 🍟🍗




Beef Nihari

Ingredients
  • 1/2 kg beef
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 2 tbsp nihari masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Method
  1. Fry onion in oil until golden.
  2. Add ginger garlic paste and beef. Cook for 5 mins.
  3. Add nihari masala, salt, and water.
  4. Cook on low heat until meat becomes tender.
  5. Mix flour with water and add for thick gravy.
  6. Cook 10 more mins.

    7. Garnish with ginger, coriander, green chili, and lemon 🍋🍲




Chicken Biryani

Ingredients
  • 1/2 kg chicken
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 onions sliced
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 2 potatoes (optional)
  • Oil
  • Green chilies & coriander

Method
  1. Fry onions until golden.
  2. Add chicken and cook for 5 mins.
  3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala. Cook well.
  4. Boil rice until 70% cooked.
  5. Layer rice over chicken masala.
  6. Add fried onions, coriander, and green chilies.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 15–20 mins.

    8. Serve hot with raita and salad 😋🍛
